Welcome to the State of the Arts 2019 survey results!

Organized on this site are the processed results from the State of the Arts survey 2019. The original survey can be viewed on its separate page, and the raw data can be downloaded on request by any artist or artist-run organization.

Please keep in mind as you explore the data that this information is not cross-referenced, so you will not be able to determine how many female respondants also identified as LGBTQ or how many Male respondants identified as African American and so on.

The raw data however (which again is freely available) can easily be cross-referenced though if you are interested in more specific aspects of the data.

Take everything with a big grain of salt and enjoy!
